Three directions for the athlete week view

All three drop emoji glyphs in favour of typographic + colour cues, lean on rounder edges than the current 2px corners, and tighten the per-day vertical rhythm. Pick whichever feels closest and I'll spec it for the actual app.

Magazine-style. Tiny date column on the left, workout as headline + meta. Hairline rules between days. Maximum content density, almost no UI chrome.
